Hello, welcome to my blog.  My name is Jim Lynn and I created this website to share information and my personal experience that you will find interesting and will benefit your journey into IT.  

I was born in Stillwater, OK, but grew up in Southeastern New Mexico.  I graduated from Eastern New Mexico University in 1979 and moved back to Oklahoma for a job and have been here ever since.

I discovered my passion for computing when taking a data processing class during my senior year of high school at New Mexico Junior College in Hobbs, NM. (Learning [BASIC](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/BASIC) on [DEC PDP-1140](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PDP-11)). I spent the last 40 years in Information Technolgy before retiring in 2018.  I still enjoy writing code even after 45+ years of coding.  

I hope that you find my blog interesting, entertaining and informative.  Please don't hesitate to reach out if you have any questions.
